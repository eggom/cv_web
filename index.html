About Me

In a nutshell

Hello. I'm Eduardo Gil and I am originally from Madrid (Spain). I trained with an MSc. in Industrial Engineering from Universidad Carlos III of Madrid. After graduation, I completed almost 12 years of career development in the manufacturing industry field, covering different operational areas within the automotive sector specifically (such as Lean Manufacturing, Quality, Procees Engineering...)

At the end of 2018, after those almost 12 greatly enjoyable years, I decided to revisit my great passion for computers, software and, in general the digital world. That was the year I bumped into this 'quite' recent field of knowledge that would enable me to join my truly great passions: software development and business acumen. That new field was labelled back then as Data Science and Big Data and it was my great opportunity to tie close the world of doing business and the digital layer.

Ask yourself if what you're doing today will guide you to what you want to be tomorrow

- Walt E. Disney

So, following the wise words of Walt E. Disney, in 2019, I decided to place a double bet by dropping my role in one of the most luxury automotive companies and pursue the lifetime dream of doing a Round The World trip while I was preparing my journey into the resteer of my career towards that amazing world.

Today, 5 years after that decision was made, I am truly embedded in the data ecosystem enjoying every step of it as, beyond my professional experience, what truly excites me in the world of data is the constant pursuit of learning and improvement. I love working on projects that challenge me and allow me to learn new techniques and tools to enhance my performance and deliver tangible results.
